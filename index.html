I'm Sorry

Through 3.5 years you've given more to me and more of yourself to me than I could ever have deserved. Every single moment that you've been mine and every single moment that I've been yours has been something from a fairytale.

I'm sorry it took me 3.5 years to understand. I'm sorry that there are still things which I no doubt do not understand. I expect nothing from you now. I can try to salvage things and I will. But should you choose to leave now, the wealth of happiness you have given me is already more than I could have asked for. I'm sorry for exacting such a heavy toll on you in return.

If you have the will then there's room to discuss things. I see a change in myself as I reflect on the years and in telling myself to work towards the future I have taken some wrong turns. With reflection I will correct the course. I just hope that it's not too late.

- Adam
